# User Guide: Getting Started with Vibe Bites Delivery

Welcome to **Vibe Bites**, the food delivery app that matches your current mood to the perfect local meal. Whether you are feeling stressed and need comfort food, or energetic and want something healthy, Vibe Bites filters local restaurant menus to deliver exactly what your body is craving.

## Core Features
*   **Mood Matching:** Select your current vibe (Stressed, Celebratory, Lazy, Focused) to view curated restaurant playlists.
*   **Smart Triggers:** Sync your wearable fitness tracker to automatically suggest meals based on your daily energy levels.

---

## How to Order Your First Mood Meal

1. Open the **Vibe Bites** app on your smartphone.
2. On the home dashboard, tap the **"How are you feeling today?"** search bar.
3. Select a mood from the animated slider (e.g., choose **Focused** if you are pulling an all-night study session).
4. Browse the filtered restaurant lists and tap **Add to Cart** on your chosen items.
5. Tap the green **Proceed to Checkout** button at the bottom of the screen.
6. Verify your delivery address, select your payment method, and tap **Place Order**.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Why isn't my fitness tracker syncing with the app?
If your daily energy levels aren't showing up on the dashboard, make sure you have granted the app permission to access your device's Bluetooth. 

**To fix this on your phone:** Go to **Settings** > **Apps** > **Vibe Bites** > **Permissions** and turn on **Nearby Devices / Bluetooth**.
